TIME TO CALL THE OPENERS!

Terrifying interdimensional aliens ravage the streets. Only this special group of teens can beat them back and seal the gates! The world government has recruited these powerful psychic teens for an experimental, elite team. Join them in the fight to take back our world!
SehaThe Hothead
SylviThe Leader
YuriThe Heart
MistelteinThe Genius

SehaStriker

Pure force, minimum effort
As the son of a famous Opener from the first Dimensional War, Seha has to live up to a lot of expectations. Seha's Phase skills and powerful sword blows might make him the strongest Opener ever—but he'd rather play video games all day than fight evil.

SylviCaster

Trained brain for massive blasts
As the Black Lambs' team leader, Sylvi works hard to prove herself as a capable and knowledgeable leader. Her Phase Force ability allows her to create psychokinetic attacks that manipulate the physical world in ways other Phase users cannot.

YuriRanger

Martial artist with abundant energy
Yuri lost both her scholarship and friends on her school team when her Phase powers manifested—later than most—during a kendo tournament. A nimble master of ranged and melee combat, she focuses on getting to the action and taking control of the situation.

MistelteinLancer

Disciple of art and war
Born in Germany, Misteltein is a stranger to New Seoul. A creative young man, he is eager to be part of the team. An innate protector armed with a mystical lance, his Phase powers buff his teammates and manipulate enemies on the battlefield.

JFighter

Carries a world of pain
A veteran of the first dimensional war, J was a bright and idealistic champion, until faced with the atrocities of war—not only from the enemy, but from within UNION, as well. J keeps his personal life a mystery, but his fighting style is straight-up fists of fury.

A MODERN TAKE ON CLASSIC GAMEPLAY

Openers is an episodic anime action RPG with a truly epic storyline that unfolds over several seasons, and wild beat-’em-up action set in the beleaguered city of New Seoul. Get to know the team’s personalities, and battle jaw-dropping monsters in classic arcade style!

A WORLD IN DANGER

Years ago, interdimensional monsters invaded the world and destroyed whole cities. Luckily, the ‘Phase energy’ emitted by their gates granted certain special humans psychic powers. Sadly, the First Dimensional War was only a precursor. Since conventional weapons have little effect, it’ll take a new generation of Openers to fight the invaders.

THE BLACK LAMBS ARE YOURS!

Join the Black Lambs and make them your perfect team. Improve their skills, gather the strongest weapons, and outfit their headquarters with state-of-the-art equipment designed to savagely beat back the invaders. Will the Openers learn to work together in time?
